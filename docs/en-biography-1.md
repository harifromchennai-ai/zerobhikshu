# **Zero Bhikshu Biography**
Our Guru's birth name is N. Devarajan. He was born in Vizianagaram (Andhra Pradesh), but he grew up and studied in Thanjavur, Srinivasa Puram. He was born on December 15, 1917. 

His father's name is T. Narasimmachari. Mr. T. Narasimmachari was a PWD Engineer and played a key role in the construction of the Pamban Bridge in Rameswaram. 

His brothers all held high positions in government and private sectors. Mr. T.N. Raghavan served as the First Chief Secretary of Tamil Nadu. Mr. T.N. Padmanabhan served as the Chairman of TVS Sundaram Finance.

**Our Guru "0" Bhikshu's Family Life:**

Our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu is a great householder yogi. Our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu, along with his good wife, seven sons, and three daughters, lived a virtuous family life. Our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu served as the Deputy Director in the Tamil Nadu Fire and Rescue Services and retired.

**Our Guru "0" Bhikshu's Spiritual Quest:**

Because our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu had a deep inclination towards spirituality from his youth, he mastered yoga practices and dedicated his body, mind, and soul to the welfare of the world.

While living a family life, drawn by spirituality, our Guru "0" Bhikshu connected with many saints and learned yoga exceptionally well. He met many saints.
