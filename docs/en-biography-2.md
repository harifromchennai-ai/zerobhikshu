Among the close disciples of Sri-La-Sri Muthu Krishna Paramahamsar, our Guru N. Devarajan was one. Before attaining Samadhi, Sri-La-Sri Muthu Krishna Paramahamsar bestowed titles upon his disciples. Looking at Mr. Devarajan, he said, "Your name is Zero." 

He further asked, "Do you know why your name is Zero?" and provided the answer: "You possess clear, undiminished, selfless wisdom. If someone bows to you once, they will elevate themselves tenfold, as if adding a zero to a one.

"If they bow twice, they will elevate a hundredfold in both spirituality and economic status. Thus, the living standards and spiritual wisdom of the people will multiply many times over. That is why I named you 'Zero'," he said.

After retiring from his high position, our Guru founded an organization called "Sri Pathanjali Maharishi Yoga Kendram" in 1986 on the upper floor of his residence in Kochadai, Madurai. The name his Guru gave was "Zero". He added "Bhikshu" to it and called himself "Zero Bhikshu".

Bhikshu means "beggar", that is, taking alms. What alms is he asking for? "I am teaching Sri Pathanjali Maharishi's Ashtanga Yoga without hiding anything. In return, I expect an alms from you.
