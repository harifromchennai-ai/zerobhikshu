"I expect from you," he said. This is the secret behind Sadguru Zero Bhikshu's holy name.

### Establishment of "Siruvar Ulagam" (Children's World):

Our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu lived his life showing honesty as his main capital. For this, he received the title of "Best Achiever" during his service from the then Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu, Mr. Kamaraj.

Throughout his life, from a young age, he kept his body healthy by doing physical exercises. The physical exercises and yoga practices he chose at the age of 6 helped him keep his body, mind, and soul healthy.

Based on the good intention that "the bliss I have attained should spread throughout this world," our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu established the "Siruvar Ulagam" in his home and taught yoga.

"Today we are the Children's World"
"Tomorrow we are the Adult's World"
"Another day we are the Divine World"

By imparting these teachings, he trained them in both Yoga and Devotion (Bhakti) paths.
