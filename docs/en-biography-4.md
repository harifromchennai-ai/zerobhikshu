<div class="biography-content" style="line-height: 1.8; font-size: 1.1em; padding: 20px;">

After our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu retired, wishing for the yoga he learned to reach all people in the world, he started an organization named "Sri Pathanjali Maharishi Yoga Kendram" in 1986 at his home in Kochadai, Madurai, in the name of Sri Pathanjali Maharishi who bestowed this yoga. Later, he taught Raja Yoga to the devotees who visited there and paved the way for them to live a yogic life. Many devotees benefited through this Yoga Kendram.

Sadguru "0" Bhikshu's service acted as a medicine for many suffering devotees. His soul power was focused on ensuring that all people in the world attain health, peace, and spiritual bliss.

In the temple belonging to the Sri Pathanjali Maharishi Yoga Kendram in Kochadai, Madurai:
1. Sarva Vallabha Valampuri Vinayagar
2. Papa Vimosana Sri Pathanjali Nagaraja
3. Arupadai Arumuga Velavanar
4. Sadasivam
5. Nandhikeswarar
6. Trishoola Sakthi

He consecrated these idols with his own holy hands and held the Guru Pooja festival for Sri Pathanjali Maharishi every year. He taught yoga practices and the path of devotion to the devotees visiting here. Thus, out of concern for the people of the world, teaching yoga and doing good, our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu attained the Lord's feet on November 8, 1997. Sadguru "0" Bhikshu's ashes were kept and worshipped in the aforementioned temple for 48 days. Following this, by our Guru's grace, a Peedam (shrine) was established for Sadguru "0" Bhikshu in the place he founded, and on June 21, 2009, a holy temple was constructed.
