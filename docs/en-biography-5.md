Kumbhabhishekam (consecration) was performed. It is a miraculous event that later our Prime Minister Modi announced that very day as the International Day of Yoga.

Since then, every year in the month of December, the Guru Pooja festival is grandly celebrated at the Sri Pathanjali Maharishi Temple located in Kochadai, and at his meditation shrine in Alagar Koil.

Daily Nithya Pooja (daily worship) is being performed at Sadguru "0" Bhikshu's meditation shrine. All visiting devotees happily share that they receive his complete grace. We humbly register that the soul of our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu has attained a body of light (Oli Sareeram) and is bestowing good health and prosperity upon all who worship him.

Let us all pray at the feet of our Sadguru "0" Bhikshu and Sri Pathanjali Maharishi, seek the Guru's grace, and pray to live a prosperous and healthy life...
Thank you!!!
